The Tower

Did you know that a gathering of giraffes is called a tower!? This ticket purchasing site is designed to do just that, allow users to buy tickets for their favorite gatherings or events. Whether they are concerts, expos, or sporting events, Tower will be there to get people together.

utilizes: Vue, Node.js, MongoDb, Auth0

Haunted Highway

Haunted Highway is an app for traveling to some of the US's most haunted locations. Planning on taking a roadtrip? Why not make it horror themed with over 10,000 haunted locations to see. Given a starting and ending location this app will return all the haunted locations along the way. If real ghosts aren't quite your thing don't worry haunted highway has you covered. Pick from a large selection of themed trips that visit your favorite movie/tv filming locations!

utilizes: Vue, Node.js, MongoDb, Auth0, Mapbox

Herd

Everyone wants to be heard so why not get to posting on Herd! Herd is a twitter clone meant to foster tight communites through instant messaging and interatcion with posts. Get recommendations on who to follow, trending topics on what to discuss, and browse other users profiles. Herd keeps you in touch with your flock instantly!

utilizes: Vue, Node.js, MongoDb, Auth0, socket.io, giphy

Keepr

Keepr is a social media site that allows users to share images and create collections. This Pintrest like app encourages users to visually share, and discover new interests by posting images to their own or others' collections.

utilizes: Vue, mySQL, C#, Auth0
